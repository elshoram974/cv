Sharikna App

Sharikna is an innovative mobile application designed for efficient data sharing and organization. It offers a user-friendly platform that simplifies data retrieval, catering to the diverse needs of both individuals and businesses on Android and Web platforms.


Key Features:

Data Sharing: Seamlessly share and organize data in an intuitive and user-friendly manner.
Efficient Retrieval: Facilitates easy retrieval of stored data, enhancing productivity and accessibility.
Group Chat: Enables users to engage in group chats, fostering collaboration and communication.
Business Integration: Tailored to meet the needs of businesses, providing tools for effective data management and communication.
User-Centric Design: Designed with the needs of both regular users and students in mind, ensuring a seamless experience for all.


Technological Approach:

Advanced State Management: Utilizes Cubit and BLoC state management for robust and scalable data handling.
Advanced Routing: Implements GoRouter for efficient navigation and routing within the application.
Clean Code Architecture: Adheres to clean code architecture principles for a maintainable and scalable codebase, ensuring long-term sustainability and flexibility.

Sharikna also includes secure login options with Google and Facebook, enhancing user convenience and accessibility. Empowering users to streamline data sharing and organization, Sharikna offers a comprehensive solution that meets the demands of modern-day communication and collaboration.

GPA PRO App

GPA PRO (MRE GPA) is an innovative mobile application that simplifies the process of calculating semester GPA or predicting it based on subject grades. The app utilizes GetX state management and the MVC design pattern to provide a seamless user experience on Android and Desktop platforms.


Key Features:

GPA Calculation: Easily calculate or estimate your GPA using the app's grade predictions.
Deep Linking: Seamlessly integrate with other apps or platforms through deep linking, enhancing accessibility and user experience.


Technological Approach:

State Management: GPA PRO relies on GetX state management to ensure smooth data flow and efficient performance.
MVC Design Pattern: It follows the Model-View-Controller (MVC) design pattern, providing strong code organization and excellent maintainability.

With GPA PRO, tracking academic performance becomes easier than ever, allowing users to focus more on their studies and educational goals.

The backend of GPA PRO is developed using PHPMyAdmin for efficient database management. Backend code.
